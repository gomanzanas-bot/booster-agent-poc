Growing Up 2040

A choose-your-own-adventure for Bay Area parents of today’s sixth graders. Five decisions. Fourteen years. One future.

Choose-your-own-adventure Bay Area AI & Education For parents
The premise

Your child is in sixth grade. The world they’ll enter at twenty-five is being built right now — by AI researchers, policymakers, and companies racing to define what work, learning, and success look like in 2040. You have a say in how they get there. If you start paying attention today.

Preview — five decisions

The questions you’ll face

Decision 01

The School Bet

Traditional track, specialized STEM program, or a new model of learning that barely exists yet. By 8th grade, the path starts to narrow.

Stay the course — or jump?

Decision 02

The AI Relationship

Assistant, collaborator, or competition. How your child learns to work alongside AI in the next six years will define what kind of work they can do.

Embrace it early — or wait?

Decision 03

The Bay Area Calculus

The most expensive place to raise a child. The densest node of future-building on Earth. The network effects are real — and so is the cost.

Stay in — or get out?
